# Persona: Urgent Local Patient

**Role:** B2C Primary User
**Type:** High-Intent, Action-Oriented

## Profile
This patient is experiencing sudden dental pain, discomfort, or acute anxiety about an upcoming necessary procedure. They are not casually browsing; they need a solution right now.

## Psychological Drivers
- **Positive Forces (What they want):**
  - Immediate reassurance that someone can help them.
  - A fast, frictionless path to booking an emergency or urgent slot.
  - Empathy and acknowledgment of their pain or fear.
- **Negative Forces (What they want to avoid):**
  - Being put on hold for 10 minutes listening to elevator music.
  - Leaving a voicemail and anxiously waiting hours for a callback.
  - Navigating complex phone trees ("Press 1 for X, Press 2 for Y") when they are in pain.

## Trigger Mapping
To successfully convert this user, the system must trigger their positive forces while mitigating their negative ones.
- **Trigger 1:** Zero-Wait Telephony (picks up instantly).
- **Trigger 2:** Dynamic State Engine (immediately validates pain/anxiety before forcing them into a rigid scheduling sequence).
- **Trigger 3:** "I'm in pain right now" context-aware hero button on the widget for an instant path to triage.
