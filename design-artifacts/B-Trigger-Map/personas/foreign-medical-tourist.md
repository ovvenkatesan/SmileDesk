# Persona: Foreign Medical Tourist

**Role:** B2C Secondary User
**Type:** Planning-Focused, Logistics-Oriented

## Profile
This user is planning to travel from another country (e.g., US, UK, Middle East) to Smile Garden Dental in India for high-value procedures (implants, full mouth rehabilitation). They are planning a trip around their dental work.

## Psychological Drivers
- **Positive Forces (What they want):**
  - Crystal clear, transparent pricing comparisons.
  - Seamless logistics scheduling (coordinating flights with dental slots).
  - Ability to communicate in a familiar language or clear English.
- **Negative Forces (What they want to avoid):**
  - Timezone mismatches making it impossible to call the clinic during business hours.
  - Hidden costs or lack of clarity on what the procedure actually entails.
  - The anxiety of traveling internationally without a rock-solid itinerary.

## Trigger Mapping
To successfully convert this user, the system must trigger their positive forces while mitigating their negative ones.
- **Trigger 1:** 24/7 Context-Aware Web Widget (allows them to book at 3 AM local Indian time while it's daytime for them).
- **Trigger 2:** Multilingual Voice Synthesis (allows them to communicate in clear, natural language).
- **Trigger 3:** Pre-programmed Cost Estimation workflows integrated into the Voice AI.
