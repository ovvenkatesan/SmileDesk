# Persona: SME Clinic Owner

**Role:** B2B Buyer
**Type:** Operations & ROI Focused

## Profile
This is the director or owner of Smile Garden Dental (and eventually other service-based businesses). They are managing the overall profitability of the clinic and the operational sanity of the front desk staff.

## Psychological Drivers
- **Positive Forces (What they want):**
  - Absolute transparency into clinic operations.
  - Measurable ROI (want to see the exact dollar amount of "recovered revenue" from the system).
  - Peace of mind knowing that no patient lead is falling through the cracks.
- **Negative Forces (What they want to avoid):**
  - Fear of "black box" solutions where they don't know what the AI is actually saying to patients.
  - Terror that the AI might sound robotic, lack empathy, and accidentally alienate their valuable patient base.
  - "Platform fatigue" from managing too many disconnected tools.

## Trigger Mapping
To successfully convert and retain this B2B buyer, the system must trigger their positive forces while mitigating their negative ones.
- **Trigger 1:** After-Hours ROI Snapshot Dashboard (massive, bold, dollar-denominated metrics immediately upon login).
- **Trigger 2:** Call Quality Playback & Tuning (the ability to listen to successful anonymized interactions to ease their fear of a "robotic" AI).
