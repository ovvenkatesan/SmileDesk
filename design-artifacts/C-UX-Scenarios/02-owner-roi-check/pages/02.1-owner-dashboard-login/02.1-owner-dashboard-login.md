## Overview

**User Situation:** It's 8:00 AM on a Monday, and the clinic owner is arriving at the office. They want to know what happened over the weekend before the morning rush begins.
**Mental State:** Hope: See clear, dollar-denominated proof that the system captured leads and recovered revenue while the clinic was closed. Worry: Facing a "black box" where they don't know if the AI missed important patient calls or failed to book emergency slots over the weekend.
**Discovery Method:** The owner opens their web browser and goes to the Smile Garden SaaS Dashboard login page, clicking a saved bookmark.

**Page Purpose:** Authenticate the owner securely using passwordless phone verification.

**Entry Context:** Bookmarked SaaS Dashboard login URL.

**Exit Action:** Owner enters OTP and submits.

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Enters registered mobile number.
- Clicks 'Send Code'.
- Receives SMS via Firebase Auth.
- Enters the 6-digit OTP code into the fields.
