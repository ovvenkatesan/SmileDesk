## Scenarios

### [01: Urgent Local Patient's Emergency Search](01-urgent-patient-booking/01-urgent-patient-booking.md)
**Persona:** Urgent Local Patient — Seeking immediate reassurance, empathy, and a fast path to pain relief.
**Pages:** Patient Web Widget
**User Value:** Instantly connects to the empathetic AI, gets their pain validated, and books an emergency appointment.
**Business Value:** A high-intent emergency lead is captured immediately without front desk intervention, reducing the missed call rate to zero.

---

### [02: SME Clinic Owner's ROI Verification](02-owner-roi-check/02-owner-roi-check.md)
**Persona:** SME Clinic Owner — Desires absolute transparency into clinic operations; wants to see concrete ROI.
**Pages:** Owner Dashboard - Login, Owner Dashboard - Home
**User Value:** Owner immediately sees the exact number of missed calls converted into bookings and the estimated dollar value.
**Business Value:** Owner feels the monthly SaaS subscription is completely justified as a profit center, preventing churn.

---

### [03: SME Clinic Owner's Quality Assurance](03-owner-quality-tuning/03-owner-quality-tuning.md)
**Persona:** SME Clinic Owner — Peace of mind knowing that no patient lead is falling through the cracks, but fears a robotic AI.
**Pages:** Owner Dashboard - Call Logs, Owner Dashboard - Settings
**User Value:** Owner listens to a recording, is impressed by the AI's empathy, and feels confident in the system.
**Business Value:** Trust in the SaaS product is solidified, reducing the risk of churn due to perceived quality issues.
