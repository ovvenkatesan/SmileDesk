# 01: Urgent Local Patient's Emergency Search

**Project:** Smile Garden Voice AI Agent
**Created:** 2026-03-15
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Verify service availability and immediately book an emergency slot without waiting on hold.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Zero Missed Opportunities (Total Lead Capture)
**Objective:** Achieve a 0% missed call rate via infinite concurrency and capture high-intent leads instantly.

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Urgent Local Patient (Primary)
**Situation:** Patient is experiencing sudden dental pain at home or work and needs immediate reassurance and an appointment, but the clinic's physical front desk is busy.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** Find a fast, frictionless path to booking an emergency slot and get immediate reassurance.

**Worry:** Being put on hold, leaving a voicemail, or navigating a complex automated phone tree while in pain.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Mobile
**Entry:** User searches for the clinic on Google, lands on the mobile website, sees the floating web widget, and clicks it because they want an immediate response.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Instantly connects to the empathetic AI, gets their pain validated, and books an emergency appointment for the same or next day.

**Business Success:**
A high-intent emergency lead is captured immediately without front desk intervention, reducing the missed call/opportunity rate to zero.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **Patient Web Widget** — User clicks the widget, selects the "I'm in pain right now" context-aware hero button, interacts with the Voice AI to express their pain, and confirms the offered booking slot. ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Persona:** Urgent Local Patient (Primary)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**
- ✅ **Want:** Seeking immediate reassurance, empathy, and a fast path to pain relief.
- ❌ **Fear:** Deeply frustrated by busy signals, long hold times, and unreturned voicemails.

**Business Goal:** Zero Missed Opportunities (Total Lead Capture)
