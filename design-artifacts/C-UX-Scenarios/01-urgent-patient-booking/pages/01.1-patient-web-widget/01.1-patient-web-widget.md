## Overview

**User Situation:** Patient is experiencing sudden dental pain at home or work and needs immediate reassurance and an appointment, but the clinic's physical front desk is busy.
**Mental State:** Hope: Find a fast, frictionless path to booking an emergency slot and get immediate reassurance. Worry: Being put on hold, leaving a voicemail, or navigating a complex automated phone tree while in pain.
**Discovery Method:** User searches for the clinic on Google, lands on the mobile website, sees the floating web widget, and clicks it because they want an immediate response.

**Page Purpose:** Validate pain and secure an emergency booking seamlessly using Voice AI.

**Entry Context:** Arrived from organic search landing on the clinic homepage and tapping the web widget.

**Exit Action:** User confirms the proposed emergency slot and closes the widget.

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Clicks the "I'm in pain right now" hero button inside the widget.
- Speaks to the AI describing their symptoms.
- Hears the AI respond empathetically and offer an appointment time.
- Vocally confirms the time.
- Receives visual confirmation inside the widget and SMS confirmation.
