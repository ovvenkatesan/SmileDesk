## Overview

**User Situation:** Mid-day during a quiet period, the owner wants to randomly audit the AI's performance to ensure it is treating patients with the intended "Neighborhood Nurse" empathy.
**Mental State:** Hope: Hear the AI successfully navigating a complex emotional patient interaction with warmth and accuracy. Worry: Fearing the AI is giving out wrong medical information or sounding cold, thereby alienating patients.
**Discovery Method:** Logged into the Owner Dashboard, clicking the "Call Logs" tab from the sidebar navigation.

**Page Purpose:** Review and playback completed AI interactions.

**Entry Context:** Clicked "Call Logs" from the main sidebar.

**Exit Action:** Clicks "Settings" in the sidebar to make a minor adjustment.

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Filters the table by "Completed Bookings" and "Call Duration > 2 mins".
- Clicks a specific log row to open the detail view.
- Plays the audio recording of the call.
- Reads the auto-generated transcript and sentiment analysis score.
