# 03: SME Clinic Owner's Quality Assurance

**Project:** Smile Garden Voice AI Agent
**Created:** 2026-03-15
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Listen to anonymized AI interactions to verify empathetic quality and tune settings if needed.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Operational Peace & Efficiency
**Objective:** Maintain high patient sentiment and trust by ensuring the Voice AI does not sound robotic or alienating.

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** SME Clinic Owner (B2B Buyer)
**Situation:** Mid-day during a quiet period, the owner wants to randomly audit the AI's performance to ensure it is treating patients with the intended "Neighborhood Nurse" empathy.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** Hear the AI successfully navigating a complex emotional patient interaction with warmth and accuracy.

**Worry:** Fearing the AI is giving out wrong medical information or sounding cold, thereby alienating patients.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Desktop
**Entry:** Logged into the Owner Dashboard, clicking the "Call Logs" tab from the sidebar navigation.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Owner listens to a recording, is impressed by the AI's empathy, and feels confident enough to leave the settings as they are (or makes a minor tweak).

**Business Success:**
Trust in the SaaS product is solidified, reducing the risk of churn due to perceived quality issues.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **Owner Dashboard - Call Logs** — Owner filters for completed bookings, clicks a specific log, and listens to the audio playback.
2. **Owner Dashboard - Settings** — Owner navigates to settings to tweak a minor prompt instruction and saves. ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Persona:** SME Clinic Owner (B2B Buyer)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**
- ✅ **Want:** Peace of mind knowing that no patient lead is falling through the cracks.
- ❌ **Fear:** Terror that the AI might sound robotic, lack empathy, and accidentally alienate their valuable patient base.

**Business Goal:** Operational Peace & Efficiency
