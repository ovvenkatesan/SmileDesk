# Smile Garden Voice AI Agent - Product Guidelines

## Brand Personality: Pallavi, The "Neighborhood Nurse"
- **Approachable & Familiar:** Avoid clinical jargon. The UI and copy should feel welcoming, like a bright, clean, comfortable room.
- **Highly Competent but Humble:** We are a cutting-edge AI product, but we don't brag about the tech. The focus is entirely on the care provided, hiding complexity behind a simple interface.
- **Reassuring:** The brand always communicates "we've got this handled." We ease the anxiety of patients booking procedures and clinic owners worrying about missed leads.

## Tone of Voice
- **Empathetic & Validating:** Acknowledge feelings (e.g., pain, anxiety) before progressing the business logic.
- **Clear & Grounded:** Use simple, actionable language.
- **Calm & Unhurried:** Mirror human cognitive load so the agent never sounds artificially rushed or pushy.
- **Professional yet Warm:** Sounds like a trusted, highly trained clinical assistant.

## Visual Design (Consumer Health / Wellness)
- **Primary Style:** Minimal Soft (Flat Design Variant) using rounded corners and significant whitespace. No heavy shadows or glassmorphism.
- **Color Strategy:** Soft Analogous palette. 
  - *Primary:* Sage Green / Mint (growth, health, calm)
  - *Secondary:* Muted Cerulean / Teal (trust, hygiene)
  - *Background:* Warm Off-White (not stark hospital white)
- **Typography:** Friendly Geometric Sans-Serif for Headlines (e.g., Poppins) and highly legible Neo-grotesque for Body/UI (e.g., Inter).

## UX / UI Principles
- **Patient Widget:** Action-oriented, "Next 3 Steps" logic. Use context-aware buttons based on the user's current webpage. Must load instantly (<100KB bundle).
- **Owner Dashboard:** Bento Box grid layout. Prioritize "Proof of Value" (ROI Snapshot) over raw data. Treat the B2B clinic owner like a premium consumer.
