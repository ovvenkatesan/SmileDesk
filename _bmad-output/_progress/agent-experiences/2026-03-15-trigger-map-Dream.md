## Step 1: Business Goals
### Layer 3: Domain Research
Verified standard KPIs for dental call answering systems. Most clinics measure success by missed call reduction and conversion rates.

### Layer 5: Self-Review
Goals are highly measurable (0% missed calls, 60% hold time reduction).

## Step 2: Target Groups
Identified four distinct groups based on the B2B SaaS model: SME Clinic Owners (Buyer), Urgent Local Patients (Primary B2C), Foreign Medical Tourists (Secondary B2C), Front Desk Staff (Internal).

## Step 3: Driving Forces
Mapped positive (wants) and negative (fears) drivers for each group. For instance, clinic owners want ROI but fear a robotic-sounding AI alienating their patients. Patients want instant relief but fear complex phone trees.

## Step 4: Prioritization
Linked the features designed in the product concept (Dynamic State Engine, After-Hours ROI Dashboard, Zero-Wait Telephony) to these specific driving forces. This validates that every feature being built directly serves an emotional/psychological need that drives the business goals.
