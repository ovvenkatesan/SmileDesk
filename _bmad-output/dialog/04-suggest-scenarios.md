### Suggested Scenarios

**01: Urgent Local Patient's Emergency Search** ⭐ Priority 1
- **Pages:** Patient Web Widget
- **Persona:** Urgent Local Patient — Seeking immediate reassurance and frictionless path to pain relief.
- **User Value:** Instant connection to an empathetic AI that validates their pain and books a slot.
- **Business Value:** Capturing a high-intent lead instantly, reducing missed calls to 0%.
- **Format:** Storyboard

**02: SME Clinic Owner's ROI Verification** ⭐ Priority 1
- **Pages:** Owner Dashboard - Login, Owner Dashboard - Home/After-Hours ROI Snapshot
- **Persona:** SME Clinic Owner — Desires absolute transparency into operations and measurable ROI.
- **User Value:** Immediate confirmation that the AI is making them money while they sleep.
- **Business Value:** Proves the SaaS value proposition, ensuring subscription retention.
- **Format:** Screen Flow

**03: SME Clinic Owner's Quality Assurance** ⭐ Priority 2
- **Pages:** Owner Dashboard - Call Quality Playback / Logs, Owner Dashboard - Settings / Configuration
- **Persona:** SME Clinic Owner — Fears a robotic AI will alienate their patient base.
- **User Value:** Peace of mind from listening to successful, empathetic AI interactions.
- **Business Value:** Builds long-term trust in the platform, reducing churn.
- **Format:** Screen Flow
