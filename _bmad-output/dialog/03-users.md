## 3. Reflection
**Saga:** Here's what I'm hearing about your Target Users (the end-users interacting with the AI):

**Primary User: The Urgent Local Patient**
- **State of Mind:** High-intent, possibly in physical pain or anxious about a procedure.
- **Goal:** Get immediate answers about service availability, find contact info, or get pricing right away without waiting on hold.
- **Frustration:** Calling a busy clinic and getting sent to voicemail when they need immediate reassurance or to book an emergency slot.

**Secondary User: The Foreign Medical Tourist**
- **State of Mind:** Planning-focused, calculating costs, potentially overwhelmed by logistics.
- **Goal:** Understand exact pricing, confirm complex service availability, and figure out travel/accommodation arrangements for both the patient and their attender.
- **Frustration:** Timezone mismatches making it impossible to call during clinic hours, and lack of immediate clarity on logistics and pricing.
